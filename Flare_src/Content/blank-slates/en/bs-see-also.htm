See-also links

See-also links link selected content in data files and memos to project items or other content selections.

This panel shows all the see-also links in the project. Double-click links to view information about the linked items or use the right-click menu to open the 'from' or 'to' files.

See-also links are displayed at the bottom of the of the 'from' file (if you can't see them, check See-Also Links in the ribbon).
