Codes

Typically, codes are themes that you identify in your data.

Codes collect, in one place, all the text, images (or portions of images), and audio and video timespans that you identify as relevant to the themes you identify.

All codes are saved here—you can create subfolders to help organize them. You can also create code hierarchies—for example, a code 'Education' could have child codes 'Primary', 'Secondary' and 'Tertiary'.
