Annotations

Annotations are notes for selected content in data files or memos.

This panel shows all the annotations in the project—double-click an annotation to open the relevant file and view the annotation at the bottom of the (if you can't see the annotations, check Annotations in the ribbon).in a popup screen beside the selected content (if you can't see the annotations, click Annotations at the top of the ).

You can create annotations for text selections, for regions within images and PDFs, and for timespans in video and audio files.
